Media container for MPEG-4 format:
Video streams: MPEG-1, MPEG-2, H.263, MPEG-4 ASP, H.264/MPEG-4 AVC
Audio streams: MPEG-1 Layers I, II, III (MP3), MPEG-2/4 (HE)-AAC, Vorbis (with privat objectTypeIndication), Apple Lossless, AC-3 (only M4V)

Advanced Audio Coding (AAC) - m4a, aac
VLC use audio codec (mp4a, mpga, m4a)
